HOW I EDIT MY YOUTUBE VIDEOES



After I've filmed some footage to the content I want to put out, I start to edit with Adobe Premiere Pro. I also use Adobe Illustrator to make my thumbnails.

For me personally, I prefer to use Premiere Pro when I edit. Before I joined my school (Zealand), I had some experience in Premiere Pro, but I have learned some new tricks and I also try to learn as well.

When I edit my thumbnails, I like to use two different programs. One of them is Adobe Illustrator and Canva.com. I mostly use Canva.com to place my photos where I want them to save time, then I use Illustrator draw and to write text because I have more creavitity in Illustrator compare to Canva.com unless I pay for it.
